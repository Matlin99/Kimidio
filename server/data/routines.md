# Daily Routines

## 07:00 - Morning Start
- Genre: Chill beats, soft jazz-hop
- Energy: Low to medium
- Purpose: Wake up gently, prepare for the day

## 09:00 - Work Focus
- Genre: Instrumental lo-fi, boom-bap
- Energy: Medium
- Purpose: Deep work, coding, reading
- Preference: No vocals or minimal vocals

## 12:00 - Lunch Break
- Genre: Soulful hip-hop, jazz rap
- Energy: Medium to high
- Purpose: Relax and recharge

## 15:00 - Afternoon Slump
- Genre: Upbeat jazz-hop, funky beats
- Energy: Medium
- Purpose: Beat the afternoon tiredness

## 18:00 - Evening Wind Down
- Genre: Mellow, emotional, nostalgic
- Energy: Low
- Purpose: Transition from work to personal time

## 22:00 - Late Night
- Genre: Ambient, atmospheric, sleep textures
- Energy: Very low
- Purpose: Relax before sleep
