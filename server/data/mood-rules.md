# Mood Rules

## Happy / Energetic
- Upbeat jazz-hop with horns and fast drums
- Funky basslines
- Artists: Digable Planets, Gang Starr, The Roots

## Sad / Melancholic
- Piano-driven beats, minor keys
- Slow tempos, emotional samples
- Artists: Nujabes (especially Modal Soul), J Dilla (Donuts)

## Focus / Concentration
- Minimal vocals or no vocals
- Steady mid-tempo beats (85-95 BPM)
- Artists: Nujabes (instrumentals), J Dilla (instrumentals)

## Relaxed / Chill
- Warm textures, vinyl crackle
- Soft drums, melodic loops
- Artists: Nujabes, DJ Okawari, Uyama Hiroto

## Nostalgic / Reflective
- Soul samples, vintage textures
- Storytelling lyrics
- Artists: A Tribe Called Quest, Black Star, Common

## Sleep / Ambient
- No drums or very soft percussion
- Atmospheric pads and textures
- Artists: Nujabes (ambient tracks), DJ Shadow (Endtroducing)
